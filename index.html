Dosage Details

Vaccine Efficacy:COVID-19 vaccines have shown high efficacy in preventing severe illness, hospitalization, and death associated with the virus. Different vaccines have different efficacy rates, but all authorized vaccines provide significant protection. Vaccine Distribution: Vaccination campaigns are typically organized by national or local health authorities. They prioritize certain groups based on factors like age, occupation, and underlying health conditions. Vaccines are usually administered in authorized vaccination centers, hospitals, clinics, or sometimes through mobile vaccination units.
